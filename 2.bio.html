hi! i’m sofia, a journalist who is always trying on new hats.

I am driven by my undying creative streak that often pulls me towards many different interests at once. Previously, I was the fall 2019 photo editor of the Daily Free Press, Boston University’s award-winning, independent publication. I enjoy film photography and have dabbled in documentary filmmaking, something I long to one day return to.

Despite my love affair with photography, writing is my lifelong devotion. I have been an editorial intern at Los Angeles and Boston Magazine—-both wonderful experiences covering the two cities that have most shaped me. While abroad in London, I wrote for online arts and culture blog Dulwich Onview and grew intimately close with the spunky South London community of Dulwich.

In the future, I hope to serve in bridge-roles where I can close the gaps between my various interests and infuse together data- and visual-driven reporting into cohesive and immersive stories. I am deeply passionate about protecting local journalism, the ways issues intersect and visibility.

I like learning new things! Most recently, I learned HTML/CSS (with the internet as my teacher, of course) and coded this website from scratch. My next aspiration is to write and code text-driven narrative games. I also like drawing and want to dabble in satirical (a reach) or heartwarming (more likely) comics.

In my spare time, I love curating playlists and listening to/watching comedy. I often lament that if I were only funnier, I would aspire to write for Last Week Tonight.

     My pronouns are she/her. You can contact me at sdkoyama (at) gmail (dot) com.
